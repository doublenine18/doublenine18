    后商 sang ooh

    Since 2014, while reading a lot, he has tried to use a delicate penmanship to present stories and ideas in what one reads and experiences.

    从2014年起，他在大量阅读的同时，尝试以细腻的笔法呈现了阅读内、世界中的故事与观念。
