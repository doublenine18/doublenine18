    后商 sang ooh

    后商 sang oohh

    sang ooh(后商) is a columnist, a salon host, and a poet.
    He has devoted to the fields of historical narrative, social welfare issue, history of ideas, contemporary art and culture.

    Since 2008, he has written more than a dozen sets of ideological projects. His words have been published in The Paper(澎湃新闻), Southern Weekly(南方周末), Phoenix Weekly(凤凰周刊), The Economic Observer(经济观察报), Beijing Document(北京纪事), Initium Media(端传媒), Sanlian Lifeweek Magazine(三联生活周刊), Beijing Youth Daily(北京青年报), Life Magazine(生活月刊), The Beijing News(新京报), Vista Story(Vista看天下), Poetry Magazine(诗刊), Literature and Art Magazine(文艺报), Beijing Evening News(北京晚报), China Philanthropists(中国慈善家), etc.

    His ancestors came from Qi(杞), Bian(汴京), in the central part of China. From 2014 to 2018, he studied at the School of Journalism and Communication of Wuhan University(武汉大学) as a library wanderer.
    He lives and works in Beijing, China.
