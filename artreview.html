    后商 sang ooh

    Since 2020, he has systematically written about art, especially contemporary art, both in China and abroad, becoming more and more systematic and serious, and some of his writings have been included in the artist's book.

    从2020年至今，他系统地书写艺术尤其是当代艺术，包罗中外，且日渐系统与切恳，部分文章收录于艺术家画册。
